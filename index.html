About Conference

This conference serves as an unparalleled platform, uniting researchers, academicians, scientists, practicing engineers, and industry professionals from around the world to discuss sustainable development and recent advancements in Computational Intelligence and its various application in the field of engineering and technology. In recent years, there has been a significant increase in interest in the intersection of Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and the Internet of Things (IoT) with sustainable development. Computational Intelligence is essential to the creation and automation of flexible information processing and services that mimic human abilities including sensing, understanding, learning, recognizing, and thinking. Intelligent and sustainable engineering solutions require ongoing innovations, research, and development to improve computational performance across the domain, analyze societal and global concerns more effectively, and generate sustainable engineering solutions. This dynamic platform target to unites visionaries, researchers, and industry professionals worldwide to explore the application of Computational Intelligence and the Internet of Things with sustainable development. From intelligent systems to additive manufacturing, our conference serves as a vibrant stage for the exhibition and thoughtful discussion of research work in the expansive field of all Engineering disciplines. Rapid breakthroughs, fueled by the demand for high-performance and energy-efficient systems, take center stage as we collectively address the pressing issues of current time under the theme "Technology and Its Promise for Sustainable Development."
